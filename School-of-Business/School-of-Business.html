ABOUT

Faculty of Business is the ideal place that we offer Business and Management degrees.These build graduates as compatible to modern Business world, full of knowledage and bussiness mind. Our university have six degree programes and they covers wide area of Business Studies.Faculty of Business create students in its methods of teaching, learning, research and networking with the business community.The students are trained for analytical skills, human skills, leadership qualities and orientations, communication skills.Further we have good library facilities for students. The Faculty of Business offers many degree programs such as Business Management, Human Resource Management, Accounting.

DEGREE PROGRAMS


We have below Degree programs for Management and Business field education.

Bsc(Hons) Business Management

Bsc Business Management is a main degree of our Management Faculty. Students can study complete syllabus of Management. THis is a four year degree. In your drgree, during the last two years you have industrial training with heighest marked industries.

Bsc(Hons) Human Resource Management

Bsc Human Resource Management is a degree, examine the concepts and theories of successful HRM. This introduce that what is the Human resource management. This is a four year degree and consist of one year industrial training.

Bsc(Hons) Marketing Management

Bsc Marketing Management is a degree programme consist of basic marketing rules and dynamic interrelationships between organizations and their clients. this is a three year degree and it has one year industrial training.

Bsc(Hons) Accounting and Finance

Bsc Accounting and Finance degree has a wide spread syllabus of marketing rules,functions and will provide a thorough grounding in financial reporting, management accounting and corporate finance. this is a three year degree and consist of two year industrial training.

Bsc(Hons) Tourism and Hospitality Management

Bsc Tourism and Hospitality Management is a covers a whole range of visitor and hospitality services, including, customer service, service development and design, front of house, food and beverage management.This is a three year degree.
