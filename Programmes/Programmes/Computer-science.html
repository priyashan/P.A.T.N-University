Bsc(Hons) Computer Science
Duration :
4-Years
Course Type :
Full Time

Year 1:
In your first year, you will learn foundations of programming to algorithms and mathematics. We have structured the curriculum to important to the industry and its areas of specialisation. You will study programming techniques, database development and inside a computing operating system.

  • Formal Foundation
  • Foundation of Mathematics for Computer Science 1
  • Computer Programming 1
  • Algorithmic Problem Solving
  • Computer Science in Practice
  • Introduction to Computer Architecture
  • Computer Programming 11
  • Foundations of Mathematics for Computer Science
  • Matrix Algebra
  • Software Engineering Project 1
Year 2:
In the second year, you will build on the knowledge you have already acquired, engaging with new subjects that will help you identify possible career paths. We will continue to focus on your employability, introducing you to the wider issues faced by IT professionals and the legislation and ethics that guide the industry. An integrating project combines all the skills you have learnt so far, and allows you to undergo a full software lifecycle, starting with a concept and ending with a product.
  • Data Structures and Algorithms 1
  • Digital Systems
  • Databases and Information Systems
  • Discrete Mathematics for Computer Science
  • Linear Algebra 1
  • Data Structures and Algorithms 2
  • Introduction to Operating Systems
  • Introduction to Functional Programming
  • Linear Algebra 2
  • Software Engineering Project 2
Year 3:
The learning in this year builds on the knowledge in the previous years of study. The course content focus on graduate employability, introducing the field of Computer Science to students in breafly. The software engineering project assigned for this year combines an assessment of all the skills.
  • Formal Foundation of Computing
  • Data Structures and Algorithms
  • Networks and Internet Systems
  • Mobile Application Development
  • Modern Operating Systems
  • Software Engineering Project 3
Year 4:
This year aims at further widening the knowledge base and skills of students by introducing modern technologies such as Artificial Intelligence, Distributed Systems and Data Mining to students with a view to deepening their understanding of subject areas like Object Oriented Design and Security. The project is designed to enhance students research skills that are essential for a career with life-long learning.
  • Distributed Systems
  • Data Mining : Theory and Practice
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Object Oriented Design
  • Computer Security
  • Software Engineering Project 4
